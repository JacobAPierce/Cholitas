Cholita’s Strikers Soccer Club was founded in 1988 as a small family oriented club. To this day, the club still operates the same, now as a non-profit, and is committed to providing the highest levels of soccer training for the youth soccer players in Wichita, KS and to enhance their social, physical, and emotional health, in an encouraging and safe environment. Cholita’s strives to construct and nurture a valuable soccer learning experience. We achieve this goal by making every effort to insure that our coaches and players develop the skills, teamwork and sportsmanship that will nurture and preserve these qualities at all levels of their soccer growth. We enable our players with the education that will allow them to demonstrate their quality as a soccer player with any team as they progress through their soccer career and develop a continuing passion for the game.
